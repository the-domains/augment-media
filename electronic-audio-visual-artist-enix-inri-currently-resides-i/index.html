Biography

Electronic Audio Visual artist ENIX INRI mutates genre with compositions which resonate with Industrial , Ambient, New Wave, Hip Hop, Avant Space Jazz and Futurist Bass Musics.

Currently residing in Philadelphia , PA. USA. is originally from Leicester, England. Initiated into the fertile underground arts and music scene at an early age. ENIX INRI cut their teeth singing, djing , rapping, painting and dancing with various sound-systems and collectives in and around clubs, coffee shops, warehouses and woodlands of the region.

Stateside gigs have included a sunrise set at a outdoor festival and a midnight session at an indie performance venue.
